# About Me
I am Jungho Lee, an incoming Research Scientist at [NAVER AI Lab](https://www.navercorp.com/main). I received my Ph.D. in [School of Electrical and Electronics Engineering](https://ee.yonsei.ac.kr/ee_en/index.do) at [Yonsei University](https://www.yonsei.ac.kr/sites/en_sc/index.do). Before joining NAVER AI Lab, I worked as a research intern at [NAVER Cloud](https://www.navercloudcorp.com) on 3D scene representation.

My research lies in 3D computer vision and visual intelligence. I am interested in building robust and scalable systems that can perceive, reconstruct, and understand the 3D world from real-world visual observations.

If you are interested in me, feel free to [contact me]({{ base_path }}/contacts.html).
